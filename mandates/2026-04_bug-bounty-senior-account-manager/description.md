We’re headquartered in Switzerland, trusted by some of the most security-conscious organisations in Europe, and scaling fast.

The Role
We are hiring a Senior Account Manager to build up and lead our commercial customer development at the highest level, owning renewals, driving expansion, and building the account management practice that will scale with our business. The Senior Account Manager independently shapes how we develop and retain enterprise customers.

You will own and build the commercial account management motion: Driving renewals, expansion, and long-term account growth so that customer value is consistently converted into predictable, sustainable revenue, measured by NRR and NDE.
