# Chief Sales Officer — BetaCorp International

BetaCorp is a PE-backed B2B SaaS platform in the HR-Tech space, serving mid-market and enterprise customers across EMEA. ARR CHF 55M, 180 FTE, headquartered in Zug.

## The Role

As **Chief Sales Officer**, you will own the full commercial motion (new business, expansion, customer success, revenue operations) reporting directly to the CEO. You will sit on the executive committee and lead a team of 45+ across 6 countries.

## Must-Have Requirements

- 12+ years of B2B SaaS sales leadership, minimum 5 years in a C-level or SVP role
- Demonstrable experience scaling ARR from CHF 50M to CHF 150M+
- Experience running multi-region sales orgs (minimum 3 countries)
- Fluent English; conversational German strongly preferred
- Private Equity / exit experience required (portfolio company, M&A exposure, IPO readiness)

## Preferred

- HR-Tech or vertical-SaaS background
- Experience building revenue operations function from scratch
- Network of mid-market / enterprise HR-buyers in DACH/UK
- Board-level communication experience
- MBA from a top-tier program

## Package

- Base: CHF 280k – 320k
- OTE: CHF 500k – 600k
- Significant equity package (management pool, exit-aligned)
- Relocation support if needed
